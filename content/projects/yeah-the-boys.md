Based on the novel by Holden Sheppard, Yeah the Boys follows Zeke, Charlie and Hammer: a bathhouse philosopher, a grieving punk musician and a closeted AFL star, all carrying damage from the same regional past. Across four explosive weeks in contemporary Perth, their lives are dragged together by public scandal, private shame, sex, grief, football, family and the fragile queer spaces that keep people alive.
