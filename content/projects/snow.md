Seven strangers arrive at an exclusive rehab in the Australian snowfields to confront their addictions. When a brutal storm cuts them off from the world and bodies start falling, it becomes clear this isn't recovery… it's retribution. Each of them was chosen. Each of them is hiding something. And whoever brought them here is determined to make them pay.
